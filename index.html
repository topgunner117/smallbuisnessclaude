08
Money · The pain everyone's felt

Invoice chasing & cash flow

✕Today

Manual, and always behind

  • Check QuickBooks and PayPal by hand for overdue invoices
  • Write reminder emails one by one
  • Guess at next month's cash position
With Claude

Drafted, tone-matched, queued

  • Scans both tools and drafts reminders matched to each customer's payment history
  • Gentle for good payers, firmer for repeat-late ones
  • Queued for your approval before anything sends
Goes further

It also builds a 30/60/90-day cash-flow forecast from AR/AP and historical payment timing — with confidence bands and flagged risks. So "will I make payroll?" gets an actual answer, not a guess.

09
Money · The Sunday-night jobs

Month-end close & tax prep

✕Today

A weekend, every month

  • Reconcile QuickBooks against PayPal settlements by hand
  • Hope nothing's missing or double-counted
  • Scramble again when tax deadlines hit
With Claude

Reconciled and explained

  • Reconciles automatically; flags uncategorized transactions, duplicate charges, and missing receipts
  • Writes a plain-English P&L narrative
Same pattern at tax time

Calculates quarterly estimated tax from year-to-date net income — or scans for contractors paid over $600 and flags missing W-9s ahead of 1099 season.

The stuff that always gets pushed to Sunday night — done Monday morning instead.

10
Time · Stop running on gut feel

Lead triage & content strategy

✕Today

Guesswork, twice over

  • Open HubSpot, scroll every lead, guess who's worth calling
  • Post whatever comes to mind, hope it moves product
With Claude

Ranked, briefed, scheduled

  • Scores inbound leads by engagement, fit, and urgency
  • Hands back a "call these 5 today" list — talking points drafted, calendar time blocked
On the marketing side

It reads your sales data to spot what's actually selling vs. what's sitting, then turns it into a 30-day content brief: what to push, what to discount, what to leave alone.

Most owners are doing both of these on gut feel right now.

How to actually turn this on

Start small, start today

1

Flip the toggle

On a paid Claude plan, open Cowork and turn on the "Claude for Small Business" toggle.

2

Connect one tool first

Don't wire up all seven on day one. Start with the one tied to your biggest headache.

3

Run it on historical data

Pick one workflow and run it once on closed or past data before trusting it live.

4

Set your approvals

Set approval preferences so nothing sends without your sign-off.
